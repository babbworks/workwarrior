# Statements — What Workwarrior Is

---

**Workwarrior is a single command that knows who you are right now.**

---

**Your work life doesn't fit in one inbox. Workwarrior gives every context its own world.**

---

**Type `p-work`. Every tool — tasks, time, journal, ledger — snaps to your work profile. Type `p-personal`. They snap back. No config files. No mental overhead.**

---

**Workwarrior is what happens when you stop fighting your tools and start making them fight for you.**

---

**Most productivity systems ask you to change how you think. Workwarrior changes where your data goes.**

---

**It's not a task manager. It's not a time tracker. It's the layer underneath all of them that makes them act like one.**

---

**TaskWarrior tracks what needs doing. TimeWarrior tracks where your hours go. JRNL records why. Hledger counts the cost. Workwarrior makes sure none of them get confused about who you are today.**

---

**A profile isn't a setting. It's a complete, isolated world — and switching costs you nothing.**

---

**Workwarrior was built for people who take terminals seriously and their time personally.**

---

**The `ww` command doesn't care which tool you need. It knows the answer is probably all of them.**

---

**Built for humans. Designed for machines. Ready for agents that manage their own work the same way you manage yours.**
